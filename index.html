02 — Role focus

Customer Support
Specialist

A Customer Support Specialist is the first point of contact for customers — resolving questions and issues through chat, email, or voice, troubleshooting problems, escalating complex cases, and working cross-functionally with product or technical teams to fix recurring issues. The role calls for clear communication, patience, technical comfort with support and data tools, and the ability to stay organized across a high volume of requests.

This is the exact work I've been doing for the past two years, just in an NGO setting instead of a tech company: resolving user issues, reconciling data across systems, meeting SLA targets, and keeping stakeholders informed — all skills that transfer directly into a remote Customer Support Specialist role.
